About Me

I am a Professor of Entomology at Sylhet Agricultural University, with a passion for advancing sustainable pest management and agricultural innovation. My research spans integrated pest management, insect-pathogen interactions, and AI-driven solutions for pest detection, aiming to enhance crop productivity in Bangladesh. With a PhD from Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University, China, and extensive experience in teaching and mentorship, I am dedicated to fostering the next generation of entomologists.

Research Interests

Integrated Pest Management

Developing eco-friendly strategies for pest control in rice and vegetable crops, emphasizing biological and cultural methods.

Insect-Pathogen Interactions

Investigating entomopathogenic fungi and bacteria for biological control of invasive pests, with applications in organic farming.

AI-Driven Pest Detection

Leveraging machine learning models, such as YOLOv5, for early detection of insect pests and crop diseases.
